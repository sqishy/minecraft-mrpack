Race Abilities RALLY

In a Rally race, every mount gets a passive ability from each of its types (one for a single-type Pokémon, two for dual-types). They stack — e.g. Koraidon (Fighting/Dragon) has both Guts and Draconic Power. There are 18, one per type.
