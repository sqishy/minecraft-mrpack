Race Held Items RALLY

Give a mount a held item and it changes how that mount races. Only items with a race effect are listed here — the rest do nothing on the track.

Rideable MountsRace AbilitiesHeld ItemsRecipesTerrainHow It Works
Arceus & Silvally — pick your ability. These change type by the plate / memory they hold, and a mount's race ability follows its type. Hold a Fairy plate and Arceus races with the Fairy ability (Pixie Drift); a Fire memory makes Silvally race as Fire, and so on. The catch: the plate/memory takes the held-item slot (and has no race effect of its own), so that mount can't also carry one of the items below. (Type: Null is always Normal — it has no type forms.)
